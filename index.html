Engram Buster!

"Is your power level the right power level...? No? Well then step right on up and bust some engrams open and get some good loot! A catch, erm... well... whats a game with out a catch. Ha ha-ha! Well then if you overpower yourself then all your gear busts. Is the risk worth the reward? I sure hope so...."

    Rules of the game.
  1. You can choose between the four engrams, each will have a random power level equivelent to its commonality
  2. You can bust as many of the engrams as you want.
  3. You will need to choose carefully because if your power level exceeds the limit, all the gear will break.
  4. Click on the engram you wish to bust and your power level will go up!
  5. Your goal is to make your power level match the limit without going over!
    • Green is a common level
    • Blue is a rare level
    • Purple is a ledgendary level
    • Gold is a exotic level
